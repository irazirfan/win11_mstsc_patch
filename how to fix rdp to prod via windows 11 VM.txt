Replace mstscax.dll + mstsc.exe


Once in Safe Mode, open CMD as Admin and run:

cmdtakeown /f C:\Windows\System32\mstscax.dll /a

icacls C:\Windows\System32\mstscax.dll /grant Administrators:F

copy /y "C:\<folder_path>\mstscax.dll" C:\Windows\System32\


same for mstsc:

cmdtakeown /f C:\Windows\System32\mstsc.exe /a
